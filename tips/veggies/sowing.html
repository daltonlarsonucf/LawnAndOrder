Direct Sowing vs Transplanting

The Correct Answer

If you are wondering if you should plant your garden from all seeds (direct sowing) or if you should buy plants and transfer them into your garden (transplanting) the answer, is it depends. This is a very frustrating answer, but it does depend on a couple of factors. Certain veggies fare better when planted from a seed meanwhile others do better transplanted. It just depends on the plant.

Direct Sowing: Pros & Cons

Pros:
- Less work than transplanting
- No greenhouse or equipment required
- The plants have better drought tolerance
- Lower costs than buying plants
- Harvest isn't delayed

Cons:
- Uses more seeds than growing transplants
- Uses more time thinning
- Might be harder to get started in too hot or cold conditions

Transplanting: Pros & Cons

Pros:
- You get earlier harvests
- It's easier to care for new seedlings indoors if the weather outside is bad
- Save on seed costs
- Works well with plastic mulch

Cons:
- Extending the season means more work
- Takes extra time caring for the starts
- Can delay a harvest due to transplant shock
