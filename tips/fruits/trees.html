Fruiting Trees

Trees That Grow Well In Florida

This list is going to be focused on our area, in Central Florida! Here we go: Apples, Avocados, Bananas, Citrus, Figs, Guava, Jackfruit, Lychee, Mangoes, Papayas, Peaches, Persimmons, and Tamarind are just a few! Some things to keep in mind is that you need more than one apple tree in order to produce fruit. It also takes years for some of these trees to bear fruit.
