Growing from Seeds vs Starter Plants

Growing from Seeds

Why you should:
• Diversity: you can order a variety of different seeds
• It is a bit cheaper
• Personal long-term satisfaction
• Less work than using a starter plant
• Keep in mind that some plants fare better when they are directly seeded into the garden

Growing from Starter

Why you should:
• It’s quicker if everything is ready
• Fruits would be produced earlier than from a seed due to being already grown
• Instant satisfaction
• Garden might start off stronger
