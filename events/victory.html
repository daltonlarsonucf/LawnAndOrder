Victory Garden Basics

Date: 

7/03/2021

Location: 

City Square Market - 1456 Plant Lane

About: 

Victory Gardens were vegetable, fruit, and herb gardens planted in private and public places during WWI and WWII. They helped supplement rations and boost morale during war times. Although we are not in a world war now, we could always use a bit of a morale boost. Come join us and learn how to create your own Victory Garden to celebrate the small victories in life.
