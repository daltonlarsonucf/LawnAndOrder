Lawn & Order is a nonprofit organization, aimed to advocate for the importance of gardening and growing your own food. Our mission is to unify and nourish the community. We hope to inspire a more sustainable lifestyle that is accessible for everyone. So let's return to our roots and grow our own food for ourselves and the Earth!
